Cheese n Veg Lasagna No Oven ब्रेड से बनाएं आसान पास्ता Cheesy Lasagna in pan

Prep Time:15-20 minutes
Cook Time:25-30 minutes


Servinges:2
Cuisine:Italian

Total Time
40-50 Minutes

Ingredients

For Toasting Bread

4 tbsp Butter (मक्खन)

8-10 Bread Slices (ब्रेड स्लाइस)


For Stuffing

1 tbsp Butter (मक्खन)

2 tbsp Oil (तेल)

1 tbsp Garlic – chopped (लहसुन)

1 medium Onion – chopped (प्याज़) ⅓

1 medium Capsicum – diced (शिमला मिर्च)

¼ cup Sweet Corn Kernels – boiled (भुट्टे के दाने)

⅓ cup Green Peas – boiled (हरे मटर)

2 fresh Green Chillies – chopped (हरी मिर्च)

1 tsp Oregano (ओरिगैनो)

½ tsp Red Chilli Flakes (चिल्ली फ्लेक्स)

Salt to taste (नमक स्वादानुसार)

1½ cups Tomato Puree (टमाटर प्यूरी)

1 medium Tomato – chopped (टमाटर)

2 tbsp Tomato Ketchup (टाॅमेटो केचप)


For Second Stuffing

1 tbsp Butter (मक्खन)

2 tbsp Oil (तेल)

1 tbsp Garlic – chopped (लहसुन)

1 medium Onion – chopped (प्याज़)

1 medium Broccoli – chopped (ब्रोकोली)

200 gm Paneer – grated (पनीर)


For Marinated Tomato

2 medium Tomato – sliced (टमाटर)

1 tsp Garlic – chopped (लहसुन)

few Coriander Leaves – chopped (धनिया पत्ती)

Salt to taste, (नमक स्वादानुसार)

½ tsp Red Chilli Flakes (चिल्ली फ्लेक्स)

1 tbsp Olive Oil (जैतून का तेल)


Process

For Stuffing

  • In a pan heat oil and butter then add garlic, onion and saute until translucent.
  • Now add capsicum, sweet corn kernels, green peas, green chillies and continue cooking for 2 minutes.
  • Then add oregano, red chilli flakes, salt, tomato puree and continue cooking until everything is cooked completely and thickened.
  • Add the chopped tomato, tomato ketchup and cook for another minute then remove and set aside for further use.
